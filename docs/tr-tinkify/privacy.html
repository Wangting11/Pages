Privacy Policy

App: TR Tinkify
Effective date: July 18, 2026
Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TR Tinkify (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the TR Tinkify iOS application (the “App”). The App includes account registration and login, as well as features that let you publish and share posts (“dynamics”). By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

1. Who we are

TR Tinkify is a social iOS application that allows users to create an account, sign in, and publish dynamics (posts) to share with others. For privacy questions, contact us at support@trtinkify.app.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of data:

  • Account and profile information. When you register or log in, we may collect information such as your email address, username, password (stored in hashed or similarly protected form), display name, avatar, and other profile details you provide.
  • Authentication data. Login credentials, session tokens, and related security information needed to keep you signed in and protect your account.
  • User-generated content (dynamics). Posts, text, images, videos, comments, likes, follows, and other content you create, upload, or interact with in the App. Content you publish may be visible to other users according to the App’s sharing settings.
  • Device and app information. Device model, iOS version, app version, language/locale, and a locally generated device identifier used for app configuration and service reliability.
  • Attribution and analytics data. Install and attribution information provided by AppsFlyer (for example, install status and media source when available) to understand how users discover the App and to operate our services.
  • Push notification token. If you allow notifications, Apple may provide a device push token so we can deliver alerts you opt into (for example, interactions on your dynamics).
  • Photos and media you choose. If you pick a photo or video for your profile or a post, the App accesses only the content you select or explicitly save. We do not browse your entire photo library without your action.
  • Purchase information. If in-app purchases are available, Apple processes payment details. We may receive transaction identifiers needed to unlock or restore purchases. We do not receive your full payment card number.

3. How we use information

  • Create and manage your account, and authenticate login sessions
  • Enable you to publish, edit, delete, and interact with dynamics
  • Display your public profile and posts to other users as intended by the App
  • Provide, maintain, and improve core App features
  • Load configuration and determine service availability
  • Measure installs and marketing effectiveness (attribution)
  • Send notifications you have enabled
  • Process and restore purchases
  • Diagnose issues, prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized access

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Other users. Information and content you choose to make public (such as your username, avatar, and published dynamics) may be visible to other App users.
  • Service providers, such as AppsFlyer for attribution/analytics, cloud hosting providers that store account and post data, and Apple for push delivery, payments, and system services.
  • Legal recipients, when required by law, regulation, or valid legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and integrity.

Third-party services have their own privacy policies. For AppsFlyer, please review their documentation at appsflyer.com.

5. Public content and social features

Dynamics and profile information you publish may be accessible to other users of the App. Please do not post personal information you do not want others to see. You can typically edit or delete your own posts and update your profile within the App. Deleting a post removes it from active display, though residual copies may remain for a limited time in backups or caches needed for security and reliability.

6. Data storage and retention

Account data and published dynamics are stored on our servers (or those of our service providers) so that you can sign in across sessions and so your posts can be shown to others. Some preferences or drafts may also be stored locally on your device. We retain account and content data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to operate the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms. You may request account deletion by contacting us; we will delete or anonymize personal data we control, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your choices

  • You can update profile information and manage or delete dynamics in the App.
  • You can deny or revoke photo library / camera access in iOS Settings.
  • You can turn notifications off in iOS Settings.
  • You can limit tracking / analytics options available in iOS Settings where applicable.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we control by contacting us.
  • You may request closure of your account by emailing us.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

9. International users

If you use the App from outside the country where our servers or service providers operate, your information may be processed in other countries that may have different data-protection laws.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated Policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, email support@trtinkify.app.
